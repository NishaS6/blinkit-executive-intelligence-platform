# Blinkit Executive Intelligence Platform

## Overview

The Blinkit Executive Intelligence Platform is an interactive Tableau Business Intelligence solution designed to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, product performance, and category contribution within Blinkit's quick commerce ecosystem.

The project provides stakeholders with a centralized view of key business metrics through executive dashboards, interactive filters, drilldowns, and product intelligence insights.

---

## Project Objectives

- Monitor overall business performance through KPIs
- Track revenue trends over time
- Identify top-performing categories and products
- Analyze brand-level performance
- Understand category revenue contribution
- Enable interactive business exploration through dashboard actions
